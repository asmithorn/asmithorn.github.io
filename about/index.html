About

I am the Creative Operations Manager who oversees the digital brand and leads the front-end design team in Web and Interactive Communications at Illinois State University in Normal, Illinois. I strive to solve communication problems and provide a smooth user experience for anyone interacting with the Illinois State brand.

Prior to working for Illinois State, I was the lead designer at a small design agency in Peoria, Illinois where I created print and web marketing materials for several clients in the health and financial fields.

After graduating with a double art major in graphic design and photography, I have continued my artistic passions in a variety of mediums. Currently, I rent a printmaking studio in downtown Bloomington where I am able to pursue my explorations of the female perspective and gender-specific stereotypes.

I am continually searching for collaborative environments to complement my creative spirit.
